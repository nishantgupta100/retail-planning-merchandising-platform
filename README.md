# Retail Planning & Merchandising Intelligence Platform

A retail planning platform designed to optimise merchandising decisions through demand intelligence, S&OP workflows and financial planning integration.

The platform enables category planners and merchandising teams to make data-driven decisions on assortment strategy, buying quantities and inventory investments.

It integrates demand forecasting, merchandising analytics and financial planning to support scalable retail growth.

## Core Capabilities

Merchandising Intelligence  
Provides SKU performance insights and assortment analytics.

Assortment Optimization  
Helps category teams select the optimal mix of products across brands and categories.

Style Grading ML  
Uses image attributes and product features to predict performance of new fashion styles.

Sales & Operations Planning (S&OP)  
Aligns demand forecasts with supply planning and operational capacity.

Financial Planning  
Enables revenue forecasting, margin projections and inventory investment planning.

Vendor Collaboration  
Supports vendor negotiations, purchase planning and supply commitments.
